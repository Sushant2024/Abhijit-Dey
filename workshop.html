Workshop and seminar

"Workshop" is an assembled group of ten to twenty five persons who share a common interest or problem. They meet together to improve their individual skill of a subject through intensive study, research, practice and discussion.

"Seminar" is a form of academic instruction, either at an academic institution or offered by a commercial or professional organization.
It has the function of bringing together small groups for recurring meetings, focusing each time on some particular subject, in which everyone present is requested to participate.
